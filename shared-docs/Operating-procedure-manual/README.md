# Operating Procedure Manual

## What's Here

This folder contains the compliance forms and operational documents for running a City of Tampa Job Order Contract (JOC). These are the forms required by the City's Division of Minority & Small Business (DMI) program and must accompany every bid and payment request.

## Understanding These Forms

### DMI-20 — Bid Subcontractor Schedule
Filed with the bid/proposal. Lists every subcontractor to be used, their Federal ID, SLBE certification status (S = City-certified Small Local Business, O = other), dollar amount, and percentage of contract. **Must be submitted with Forms 10 and 50 or bid is non-responsive.**

### DMI-30 — Payment Period Subcontractor Report
Filed with every invoice/payment request. Reports what each sub was paid during that period. Failure to sign = non-compliance under Ordinance 2008-89.

### Gordian
Gordian is the JOC software platform used by the City of Tampa to generate work orders, price tasks using a unit-price book, and manage contract administration. The `To Do in Gordian.docx` file contains internal procedure notes for working within it.

## Pain Points Observed

- DMI-20 and DMI-30 forms are being filled out, saved as PDFs, and stored here manually — no tracking of which forms have been submitted vs. are pending
- The `To Do in Gordian.docx` checklist is the only documentation of internal workflow — if lost or forgotten, the process has to be reconstructed from memory
- No master list of which projects are at which DMI compliance stage
- SLBE percentages must be calculated manually each time — error-prone under deadline pressure
- Phase naming is inconsistent ("Phace" vs "Phase" in filenames) — signals file management is ad-hoc
